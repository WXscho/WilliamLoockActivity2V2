Esports History

First esport event - 1972

On Octuber 19th, 1972, the first esports tournement for the game called spacewar was held at the University of Stanford.

Esports emerges in South Korea - 1997

The Red Annihilation Tournement for Quake held in South Korea after the emergence of community computer labs. These labs were established during South Korea's modernization efforts toward developing internet infrastructure. Resulting in communities of gamers coming together to play in micro competitions. Eventually leading to a The Red Annihilation Tournement won by Dennis "Tresh" Fong.
